Semantics

    

        Semantics is the study of meaning. It focuses on the relation between signifiers , such as words, phrases, signs, and symbols, and what they stand for, their denotation.
    
 
    

        In linguistics, semantics is the subfield that is devoted to the study of meaning, as inherent at the levels of words, phrases, sentences, and larger units of discourse.
